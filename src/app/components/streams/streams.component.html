Starting a stream - kat-nx

I've been thinking of starting a stream for quite some time now. I think it was when I completed my equipment of a decent camera and decent microphone. The time has come. Check out my twitter and twitch for announcements and schedule.

Theme

The theme of my stream is - what else - live coding. But not just live-code anything. Live coding on Nx, and more specifically on the Nx Storybook plugin.

Nx GitHub-reported issue

Some sessions may include solving/debugging/live-coding on an issue reported on the GitHub Nx repository. I may or may not give viewers the chance to vote for the issue/bug they want me to look at during the streaming session. I will be focusing on Nx+Storybook issues/bugs, first and foremost. And if there is no particular issue/bug to focus on, there's always some feature request, or a feature I'm thinking to implement.

Walkthrough of a new feature or a how-to guide

In some cases, a new feature comes up. Or a new way of doing something comes up. Or something changed, and users may need some guidance. The stream, that day, will be dedicated to, either the walkthrough of this new feature, or the how-to guide for doing something. For example how to migrate to a newer version, or how to use some other technology with Nx + Storybook.

Logistics

Frequency

I will target for a frequency of once-a-month, with a highly overoptimistic of once every two weeks. So either monthly or biweekly. Some dates may be skipped due to holidays, but generally this is the plan, as I'm setting it out now.

Timezone friendliness?

The stream will be going live at 20:00 my time (Greek time), which is 10:00am Pacific Time, 13:00 Eastern Time.

Length

One hour. It may not be enough to solve a bug/issue, but it will be enough for the viewers to get any value out of it.

Platform

I will be using Twitch. After the broadcast, the videos will be available on my YouTube channel.

Communication

My Nrwl email is always open, but I also have a Discord (which I do not check frequently - it confuses me - I'm old - but I will be checking during the streams).
